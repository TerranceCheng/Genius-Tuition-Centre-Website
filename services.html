Services

Our Services

Physical Classes

We have 2 major lecture halls, 5 tuition rooms and
2 meeting rooms for students to join each other
and discuss anything about their homework,
lecture notes and so on.

Online Classes

Since the MCO had started, our tuition centre had
introduced the online learning method to all our
students.

Our teachers will provide all the study materials to
the students through online platform, such as google
classroom. All the tutors, homeworks and notes will
be discussed during the online meeting is conducted.
So that the parents would not be worried about their
childrens' safety, and they would be able to learn
comfortablely from their home.

Transportation

Our tuition centre provides transportation services to
all the parents who do not have the time to send their
children to our tuition centre.

With our well-trained drivers, your children are
guarantied to reach their destination safely.

If you are interested to apply for the transportation
services,please send an email to us or contact us
through our phone number to complete the registration.

NOTICE : Transportation services is currently being postponed due to MCO
                restriction ( Movement Control Order ).
