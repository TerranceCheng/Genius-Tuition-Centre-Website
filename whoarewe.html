Who Are We

Who are we

Genius Tuition Centre started up in 2010. We understand
the importance of academic performance for all students.
Therefore, we aim to provide quality education and
excellent service to all of our students.

We strongly believe that every child is a winner. Hence,
we put in all of our effort to maximize each student's
possibilities and guide them to achieve their goals in
academic.

Objective

  • To make sure all the students are able to keep
    up with their lessons in this pandemic or even
    after this pandemic.

  • To make sure all the students are well prepared
    before they further to tertiary education.

  • To cultivate new generation with balanced abilities
    in all aspects.

Our learning environment

We make sure all the students are able to learn in the
best environment which are comfortable and safe. In our
classes, all the students will be able to provide their own
feedbacks or ask questions without worrying about taking
risks or making mistakes.

We make sure our teachers are treating the students like
their own child. There are nothing better than upgrading
yourselves while enjoying the progress.
